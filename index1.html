How it works

Green Cover Detector is designed to calculate green area in any part of the world. Using Google Earth Engine and Numpy with Python, Satellite Imagery is taken from Landsat-8 and Copernicus.

Click Get Started button and world map will pop-up.
Select desired coordinates with the help of drawing toolbar, most recommended are Rectangle and Polygon then click Submit.
Click Process, processing may take a while so please be patient.
You'll see Green Area, Built Area, Green Certification and Recommendations.
You can use the same technique to find Green Area in any part of the world.

Users are requested to select one area at a time, selection of city may cause app to crash.
